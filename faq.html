What is the main purpose of WellNest?

WellNest is designed to help caregivers manage their patients’ care more efficiently by organizing tasks, medication schedules, and doctor’s appointments. It provides an easy-to-use interface to ensure caregivers are informed of their responsibilities, reducing stress and confusion.

What types of tasks can I track in WellNest?

You can track a variety of caregiving tasks in WellNest, including daily responsibilities, doctor’s appointments and medication schedules. WellNest allows you to organize, delegate and view these tasks on a calendar.

Does WellNest provide medical advice?

No, WellNest does not offer medical advice. It is not intended as a substitute for professional caregiving services or healthcare consultations. It helps caregivers organize and track tasks but cannot provide personalized medical guidance or diagnosis.

Can WellNest replace professional caregiving services?

WellNest is a caregiving organizational tool and is not meant to replace professional caregiving services. While it helps streamline caregiving responsibilities, it cannot provide hands-on care or professional healthcare services.

Can I share tasks between multiple caregivers?

Yes! One of the main features of WellNest is the ability to delegate and share caregiving tasks between multiple users. This promotes better communication and collaboration among caregivers.

Is WellNest available on all devices?

WellNest is currently a desktop application designed for use on computers. While the application is intended to provide caregiving assistance on desktop platforms, mobile app versions may be introduced in the future to improve accessibility.

What if I miss a medication dose?

WellNest provides a graphical representation of missed or taken medication dosages. This feature helps caregivers keep track of the patient’s adherence to prescribed medication. However, it is important to consult with a healthcare professional for medical advice if doses are consistently missed.

Does WellNest sync across multiple devices?

At this time, WellNest is a desktop application. If you need the ability to access your caregiving information on multiple devices, please check back for future updates, as we plan to introduce cross-device sync in upcoming versions.
